Our Mission

The CS50 political map is dedicated to informing voters about their elected officials and what is occurring in politics today. In the last midterm election, six out of ten eligible voters did not even show up at the polls. We believe that our project can engage and educate the American voter, so that they can become effective participants in our democracy.

What We Do

Our site provides voters with information regarding election polls, political news and much more. Users are able to either search for a certain district, or our website will automatically detect their location. We hope that this will entice users to take an interest in local politics and political news that truly pertains to them

How We Do It

We provide this material in an interactive and engaging fashion in order to facilitate increased participation across all demographic groups. The online format specifically targets younger voters, millions of whom lack any interest in the political process.
